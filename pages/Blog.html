Solo Travel Wisdom

This table below will give you a brief idea of the taxi fares in and around Shillong (2019):

Shillong sightseeing Full day: 2000 INR
Half Day: 1300 INR
Shillong to Mawlylong Both ways: 3000 INR
Shillong to Cherrapunjee One way: 2000 INR
Whole day package: 2500 INR

On average, a Shillong trip for 4 days (from Guwahati) will cost you around 8000-10000 INR. You can cut down on the costs if you travel in a group.
